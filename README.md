## About the app
Friendly Tennis Match is an Apple Watch app for tracking tennis match scores.
The Apple Watch app works together with the iPhone app, where users can review detailed match statistics after each match.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does the app require an account?
No. The app does not require sign in or user accounts.

### What devices are supported?
The app requires an iPhone and an Apple Watch.

### What data does the app track?
Match scores and workout data such as heart rate, steps, distance, calories, and match duration.
